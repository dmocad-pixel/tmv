01 — Investment Framework

Thesis Patterns from Portfolio Analysis

Derived from analyzing Marina Hadjipateras's led investments across TMV Fund I and Fund II.

01

Visibility & Analytics First

TMV consistently backs platforms that transform fragmented maritime data into actionable intelligence. Not marketplaces—software that creates transparency.

Evidence: Portcast (ETA prediction), Nautilus Labs (fleet analytics), Trackstar (API visibility)
02

Seed to Series A Sweet Spot

$500K–$2M checks into companies with product-market fit signals but pre-scale. Marina's operational background enables hands-on value-add at this stage.

Evidence: Led OpenTug Seed ($2.2M), Portcast Pre-A ($3.2M), Secro Seed
03

Sustainability as Efficiency

Not ESG theater—investments where carbon reduction is a byproduct of operational efficiency. Fuel savings = cost savings = emission reduction.

Evidence: Nautilus Labs (route optimization), Portcast (slow steaming insights)
04

Global Trade Infrastructure

Companies building digital rails for international shipping: documentation, crew logistics, port operations. Problems Marina understands from Dorian LPG.

Evidence: Secro (eBLs), Greywing (crew changes), Harbor Lab (port costs)
